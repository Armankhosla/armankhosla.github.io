About

Raj Arman Khosla is the Founder of Enlightenment Care Charitable Trust and an educator working to bring structure, clarity and encouragement to students from diverse backgrounds. His approach focuses on discipline, personal guidance and consistent academic mentoring.

Along with academic involvement, he contributes to community building and youth leadership within church and social groups. His leadership style is responsible, fair and focused on maintaining a positive environment where people can work with unity and respect.

Raj is committed to shaping systems that run smoothly, building stable charitable efforts and creating opportunities for young people to develop confidence and a future-ready mindset.
